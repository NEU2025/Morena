New Era University

Rationale

Many school libraries rely on outdated manual systems for borrowing and returning books. These systems lead to inefficiencies, misplaced books, and overdue penalties due to human error and poor tracking. The NEURead addresses these issues by integrating RFID technology to automate and optimize library operations, reduce staff workload, and enhance the student experience.

Objectives

  • Automate the borrowing and returning of books.
  • Provide real-time book tracking and history logs.
  • Send email reminders for due dates to reduce penalties.
  • Improve inventory control and library management efficiency.
  • Enhance accessibility and usability for students and staff.

Conclusion

The NEURead system effectively addresses the challenges faced by traditional library management systems. By leveraging RFID technology, it streamlines the borrowing and returning process, enhances tracking accuracy, and reduces manual errors. The system's user-friendly interface and automated notifications significantly improve the overall library experience for both students and staff.
